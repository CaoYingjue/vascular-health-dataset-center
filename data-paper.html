Data Papers

Dataset Descriptors and Data Resources for Vascular Research, Artificial Intelligence, and Digital Health

Data Papers are peer-reviewed scholarly articles that describe high-quality datasets, including data acquisition protocols, annotation methods, quality control procedures, data structure, and potential applications.

Unlike traditional research articles, Data Papers focus on making valuable datasets discoverable, reusable, and citable. Each published Data Paper is linked to an associated dataset and serves as the primary reference for researchers utilizing the resource.

The Vascular Health Data Paper collection aims to promote transparency, reproducibility, and innovation in vascular medicine, medical imaging, artificial intelligence, and translational research.
